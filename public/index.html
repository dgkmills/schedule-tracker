About My Classes & Projects

Detailed Class Descriptions

Biz Kid$

My flagship business-focused ESL class makes learning English both fun and practical. Students are immersed in a gamified learning environment where they start their own pretend businesses. Key activities include:

  • Designing products and marketing materials.
  • Practicing conversational English for sales and customer service.
  • Developing presentation skills and managing pretend finances.
❮ ❯

Web Kid$

An innovative course where students combine English language skills with modern web development. Using platforms like Gemini for assistance, students learn to:

  • Build personal websites with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Create content like descriptions, stories, or blog posts in English.
  • Develop a professional online portfolio showcasing their language and technical skills.
❮ ❯

Professional English

This curriculum is designed for businesses and adults seeking to improve workplace communication. The course is tailored to your company's needs, focusing on:

  • Writing effective and professional emails.
  • Leading successful meetings and negotiations.
  • Delivering clear and confident presentations.
